# Privacy Policy for GPSPY

**Last Updated: 2026-03-15**

GPSPY ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
GPSPY is an **offline-first** application. 
- **Location Data**: The app collects location data (latitude, longitude, altitude) to record your movement trajectory. This data is collected in the background if you start a tracking session.
- **Local Storage**: All recorded data is stored locally on your device.

## 2. How We Use Information
We use your location data solely to provide the tracking functionality and to allow you to export your tracks as GPX files.

## 3. Data Sharing
**We do not collect, transmit, or share your data with any external servers.** Your data stays on your device unless you manually choose to export and share it via other apps.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
